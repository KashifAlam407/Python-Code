Achievement

MEMBER OF USO & UNESCO

Vatayan School became a member of both the united Schools Organization (UNESCO) in 1986. Since then the school has been facilitating the three exams organized by USO every year and many students have received merit certificates and monetary awards as well. All India Inter School festivals are held by USO annually where we have been a regular award-wining participant.

RAM RAJ SCOUTS AND GUIDES

Vatayan School is one of the first schools is Siwan to have become a member of the national organization, Bharat Scouts and Guides (BSG), with the formation of its Ram Raj Scout and Guides in 1991. In 1993, BGC presented the Rashtrapati Scouts/ Guides Award to our students Manoj Pandey, Dharmendra Kumar, Jaya Shila and Narmadeshwar.
As part of scout & Guides training, camps are arranged where students are taught self-help and social skills.
